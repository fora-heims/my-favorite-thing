Describing What A Fox Is

Foxes are mammals of the Canidae family. They have a long bushy tail and triangular ears that are perched upright. Humans fascination with foxes can be seen in many places within modern and ancient culture.

Reasons Why I Think Foxes Are Awesome

  • Foxes come in many beautiful colors
  • Foxes are very playful and social
  • Foxes are quick and agile
  • Foxes are very adaptable
